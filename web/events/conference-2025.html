Structured Data for AI & SEO

Event Overview

The LLM Profiles Conference 2025 brings together experts in structured data, SEO, and AI to explore the future of machine-readable content. Learn how to bridge the gap between search engines and large language models.

Event Details

Date: TBD 2025

Location: Virtual Conference

Duration: 2 days

Audience: SEO professionals, developers, AI/ML engineers, content creators
